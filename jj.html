Xoom offers a wide range of car insurance policies, including comprehensive coverage, third-party liability, collision coverage, and personal injury protection. Our plans are designed to be flexible, ensuring you find a policy claims hotline or logging into your account on our website.

To file a claim, contact your Xoom as soon as possible after the incident. Gather all necessary documents such as receipts, photos, and reports. Submit your claim online, through an app, or by website, depending on your provider. Keep track of your claim number and follow up if needed. Roadside assistance is not always included but can often be added as an optional coverage. It typically covers services like towing, flat tire changes, jump-starts, and lockouts. Always check your policy or ask Xoom directly to confirm coverage.
